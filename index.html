The Astra Mythos

Founded in 2024 by two visionaries, Aaditya Thakur and Abhinav Guleria , who converged under the pen name Dr. Artistery, Astra stands as more than just a studio. We are keepers of an ancient flame, rekindled in digital realms.

Our craft fuses Vedic aesthetic principles with prophecies of tomorrow. Each piece we forge carries whispers of the past and echoes of futures yet unknown. In the intersection of darkness and light, we craft revelations.
